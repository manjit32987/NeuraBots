Yantrika

Overview

Yantrika is a compact mobile robot built for smart home automation and environmental monitoring. It can move autonomously around your house and display real-time air quality metrics on a built-in screen.

Key Features

  • Air quality, humidity, temperature, and CO sensors
  • ESP32 powered microcontroller for wireless control
  • Mobile app connectivity for navigation and data
  • Display screen to show real-time environmental stats
  • Rechargeable battery and smart charging dock support
